Love going is a creative duo formed by +777000 and nunez. Using TidalCycles and Hydra, +777000 and nunez collaborate to create club-focused algorave performances driven by the fun, the weird, the naive, and the familiar. The work incorporates elements from online culture both musically and visually, striving for a balance between going hard and going cute. Expect samples, stock images, memes, dusty pop songs, and the feeling of watching YouTube at an afters with your friends.
